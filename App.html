Propositional Logic

Generate all the normal forms for a given formula

Instructions

Conjunction (And): ^ (caret) or ∧
Disjunction (Or): v (lower v) or ∨
Negation (Not): ! (bang) or ¬
Implication: -> or →
Equivalence: <-> or ↔

Example: !A v B <-> (A -> B)

Normal FormFormula
CNF(¬A ∨ B ↔ A → B) ∨ ¬(¬A ∨ B ↔ A → B)
DNF(¬A ∨ B ↔ A → B) ∨ ¬(¬A ∨ B ↔ A → B)
NNF(¬A ∨ B ↔ A → B) ∨ ¬(¬A ∨ B ↔ A → B)
